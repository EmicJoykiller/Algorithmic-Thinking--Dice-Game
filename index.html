Dice Game Master

Game Rules

  • There are six rounds in total
  • You can roll the dice up to three times each round
  • Start by rolling the dice
  • Select a score or roll again
  • If you select a score, proceed to the next round
  • If not, you can roll up to two more times

Scoring

  • Three of a Kind: Sum of all five dice
  • Four of a Kind: Sum of all five dice
  • Full House: Three of a kind and a pair (25 points)
  • Small Straight: Four consecutive dice (30 points)
  • Large Straight: Five consecutive dice (40 points)
